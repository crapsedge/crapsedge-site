🎲 Explore Craps Systems

These systems use real math, reduce risk, and give you more control at the table. Explore each one — then test them with your own discipline.

🎯 1. The Iron Cross

Bet on the Field and Place the 5, 6, and 8. You win on almost every number except 7. Fast-paced, but dangerous if you stay too long.

🔄 2. 3-Point Molly

A smart combo of Pass Line + Odds + 2 Come Bets with odds. Keeps 3 numbers working. Great for disciplined players using odds properly.

💰 3. Pass Line with Odds Only

Low house edge, slow and steady. Always take full odds behind your line bets. It’s boring — but it keeps you in the game.

🧱 4. Don't Pass Hedge System

Bet Don’t Pass, but use small bets on the Field or Place numbers to reduce boredom and cover losing streaks. Great for grinding.

📊 5. 10% Bankroll Stop-Loss

Always walk away if you lose more than 10% of your session bankroll. Forces discipline. Protects emotions. Keeps you in control.

🎯 6. Controlled Regression Strategy

Start with 3 Place bets, and regress after 1 or 2 hits. Lock in profits and reduce risk. Especially effective with a hot shooter.

⚡ 7. Power Press

Let your bets ride and press aggressively after every hit. High risk, high reward. Use this only when you’re up and feeling confident.

💼 8. CrapsEdge Hybrid Approach

Use a mix of Iron Cross, Odds-Only, and 10% Stop-Loss depending on table conditions. Adapt to survive. Smart players switch gears.
