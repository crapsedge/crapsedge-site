‚úÖ The Best Craps Bets for Smart Players

The optimal strategy involves exclusively playing the three lowest-edge bets and maximizing your Free Odds. This is where you limit the casino's mathematical advantage:

  • **The Pass Line (1.41%):** The entry point to almost all craps play.

  • **Free Odds (0.00%):** This bet has zero house edge, making it the mathematical holy grail of craps. You must maximize this bet to lower your **effective** house edge to well below 1%.

**Pro Tip:** Your ultimate goal in craps is to reduce your average house edge to **0.5% or less**. This requires consistently maximizing Free Odds bets (often 3x, 4x, or 5x your line bet).
